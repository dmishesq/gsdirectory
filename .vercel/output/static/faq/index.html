Global Site Directory FAQ

What types of data do listings include for each business?

Each listing you submit includes the core business details you provide through our submission form: city, state, phone number, and a business description. Every listing also includes a live link to your website — a real dofollow backlink — and is categorized so customers can find you by service type. After you claim a listing, it can receive a verified/claimed badge to show ownership. Our team reviews every submission within 24–48 hours to ensure the information is accurate before approval.

Do all listings receive dofollow backlinks, and are there any restrictions on those links?

Yes — every approved listing includes a real dofollow backlink to your website. Our listings are free and permanent, and the dofollow link is a standard part of each listing. Listings are reviewed by our team during the 24–48 hour approval process to make sure submissions meet our listing guidelines. The core guarantees are that listings remain permanent on the directory and include real dofollow links as part of the listing package.

What is the verified/claimed listing badge, how do I qualify, and what are the benefits?

The verified/claimed badge shows that a business has claimed ownership of its directory listing and that the information has been reviewed by our team. To qualify, submit your business using our submission form and then claim the listing when prompted; our team will review the claim as part of the 24–48 hour verification process. The benefits of claiming and earning the verified badge include an official marker of ownership on your listing that helps build customer trust and signals to visitors that the information is confirmed. Verified listings also make it clearer which contact details and website links are maintained by the business owner.

Is the online directory available worldwide, and are there regional differences in coverage?

Our directory serves a global audience — businesses from any country can submit listings. Because we accept listings worldwide, coverage reflects the businesses that submit and claim listings across different regions. Listings are indexed for discoverability, so entries from any location can be found by search engines once approved. If you are planning to list multiple locations, submit each location with its city and state (or regional equivalent) to ensure accurate local visibility.

If you are ready to add or claim a listing, visit our submission page and complete the form in under two minutes — our team will review your submission within 24–48 hours. We are here to help you improve discoverability and get a permanent, dofollow backlink to your site.
